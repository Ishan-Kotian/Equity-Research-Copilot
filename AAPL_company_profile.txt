Apple Inc. is a global technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. 
It designs, manufactures, and sells consumer electronics, software, and services. 
Its core products include the iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, and AirPods.

Apple also generates significant revenue from digital services such as the App Store, 
iCloud, Apple Music, and Apple TV+. The company emphasizes ecosystem integration across 
devices and services.

In its most recent quarter, Apple reported revenue of $119 billion, up 2% year-over-year. 
Growth was led by iPhone and Services, while Mac and iPad segments declined. 
Gross margin expanded to 45% due to favorable mix and higher services revenue.

Apple continues to invest heavily in artificial intelligence, semiconductors, and ecosystem 
expansion, positioning itself for long-term growth.
